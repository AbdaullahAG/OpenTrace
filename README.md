Most people have no idea their feed is a filter bubble. OpenTrace lets you upload your data export file from YouTube or TikTok, then shows you a clear visual breakdown — how diverse your sources are, whether your content leans in a political direction, and what manipulation patterns the algorithm is using on you. Everything runs locally using Ollama and Gemma 2, so nothing gets sent anywhere. At the end, it suggests open-source alternatives based on your usage pattern.
